# 10 Second Cinema: Doctor WTF

 - published: 2016-03-18 23:00

In this episode I wanted to learn more about Motion and play with lighting effects.

To try and help sell the effect of the bridge lights being turned off we used
a mixture of real and fake lighting. The green glow of the sonic screwdriver is also
both real and and enhanced.
